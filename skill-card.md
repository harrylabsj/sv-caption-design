## Description: <br>
Covers caption strategy - styling, placement, timing, accessibility, and engagement - for short video subtitles and on-screen text. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[harrylabsj](https://clawhub.ai/user/harrylabsj) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
External video editors, content creators, social media managers, and accessibility-focused creators use this skill to plan short-video caption style, placement, timing, and accessibility checks. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Users may expect automated transcription, caption generation, rendering, or video processing from this skill. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the skill as informational caption design guidance only and use separate reviewed tools for transcription, rendering, or video processing. <br>
Risk: Incorrect caption text or translation choices can reduce accessibility or mislead viewers. <br>
Mitigation: Review caption accuracy, translation quality, timing, and accessibility requirements before publishing a video. <br>
